Agreement status and notifications

Agreement inactive – if the user does not perform the payment by the end of the Purchase Agreement issue date, the current agreement becomes inactive. No notification will be sent to the User.    

Agreement active - after the payment execution by the User with the use of the Vaulter Payment Link to the Vaulter’s vault, Vaulter will send a notification via email to the User confirming the payment. The notification will also include the current Payment Agreement that became active after the payment.   

Agreement closed - at the end of the delivery day for the item/ service (mentioned above), Vaulter will send a notification email to the User as confirmation for releasing the safeguarded amount to the Company’s bank account and the closure of the current Payment Agreement. The closure of the agreement notification will include a payment report. 

Agreement canceled - in case of the cancelation of the acquisition of the item/service (mentioned above) by either User or Company, Vaulter will send a notification via email to the User mentioning the cancelation of the current Purchase Agreement and the reimbursement of the safeguarded amount (less the administration/ cancelation fee perceived by the Company, if applicable) to the bank account from which the initial payment was performed. The cancelation notification will include a payment report.  

 

Agreement cancelation

If the User wants to cancel the acquisition of the item/ service (mentioned above), he needs to contact the Company and make this request.

Depending on the item/ service cancelation policy regulated by the Company’s Terms and Conditions and business terms, the amount safeguarded in the Vaulter vault will and can be refunded to the User in case of cancelation, only with the Company’s approval. The value of the amount reimbursed to the User from the Vaulter vault can be different from the safeguarded amount if the Company is perceiving an administration/ cancelation fee according to its own Terms and Conditions and business terms.

The reimbursement is made to the same bank account from which the User performed the initial payment to the Vaunter’s vault, and is executed at the moment when the Company sends the cancelation approval and details to the Vaulter Payment Link service. The communication between the Company and the Vaulter Payment Link service is done through API integration to ensure data protection and fast execution of orders.

If the User has any claims regarding the actual payment performed with the Vaulter Payment Link, he needs to contact Powers AB via email at quaries@vaulter.se within 3 months from the moment of the payment, or they have no rights.
